Our Story

Born in a Garage. Grown into a Movement.

It started with one roaster, one bag of Ethiopian beans, and an obsession with finding the perfect cup. Fifteen years later, Roast Coffee Co. is the most awarded artisan coffee brand in the Pacific Northwest.

We source directly from 40+ farms across 12 countries, building relationships that ensure every bean has a story worth tasting. Our master roasters treat each batch like a work of art.

How We Roast

The Process Behind the Cup

01
Direct Sourcing
We travel to the farms. Meet the farmers. Taste the cherries off the branch. No middlemen.
02
Green Selection
Every bag of green beans is hand-inspected and cupped before it earns a place in our roastery.
03
Small Batch Roasting
Max 15kg per roast. Our master roasters monitor every second of the Maillard reaction.
04
Rest & Ship
Beans rest for 48 hours post-roast, then ship to you within 24 hours. Peak freshness, guaranteed.
